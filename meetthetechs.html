Getting Started with Workplace Services

Download the App

To access our essential services, download the Workplace app. With just a few clicks, you can raise a request for plumbing, electrical, or any other home maintenance services. Our app is simple to use and ensures quick service delivery.

Set Your Location

Once you’ve downloaded the app, simply enter your location to let us know where you need the service. Whether you need a plumber or an electrician, we’ll ensure that a skilled worker arrives at your doorstep promptly.

Raise a Service Request

With just a few taps, you can raise a request for the services you need. Whether it’s plumbing, electrical work, or any other essential home service, our platform connects you to the nearest available professional ready to assist.

Skilled Workers at Your Service

Our app connects you with skilled and verified workers, such as plumbers and electricians, based on your location. They will arrive at your doorstep, ready to complete the task efficiently and professionally.

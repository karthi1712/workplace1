Careers

Role:

Coordinate schedules and dispatch skilled professionals like plumbers, electricians, and tutors to service requests from clients in a timely manner.

Skills Needed:

  • Strong organizational skills, communication, and basic understanding of service workflows.
  • Experience in scheduling and client communication tools.

Role:

Monitor and ensure the quality and reliability of services offered by our providers through customer feedback, on-site checks, and reviews.

Skills Needed:

  • Attention to detail, familiarity with service standards, and customer service experience.
  • Basic technical knowledge of services like plumbing, electrical, or academic tutoring is a plus.

Role:

Help onboard new service providers, verify qualifications, and conduct orientation sessions to ensure quality and consistency in service delivery.

Skills Needed:

  • Excellent communication and training skills.
  • Experience with onboarding, background checks, and compliance.

Audio and Subtitle Languages

Audio Languages

English As a widely understood global language, English can help reach a broad audience and allow women from different regions to participate in the courses.
Hindi As one of the most spoken languages in India, offering courses in Hindi can make learning accessible to millions of women across the country.
Telugu Since you’re based in Andhra Pradesh, Telugu would be an excellent option to connect with local women in their native language, enhancing accessibility and engagement.
Tamil Tamil is another widely spoken language in southern India. It would allow women from Tamil Nadu and surrounding regions to join comfortably.
Bengali With many Bengali speakers in India and Bangladesh, adding Bengali as a language option can extend the reach of the platform to more women.
Kannada Kannada is essential for learners in Karnataka and would make the platform inclusive for those who are native Kannada speakers.
Marathi Including Marathi will help you reach women in Maharashtra and other Marathi-speaking areas, broadening your platform’s impact.
Punjabi Punjabi can reach women from Punjab and nearby regions, enabling a sense of cultural connection in learning.
Urdu Urdu can appeal to speakers in parts of India and Pakistan, providing inclusivity for a larger South Asian audience.
Gujarati Offering courses in Gujarati can cater to learners from Gujarat and help them better engage with the content.
Malayalam Including Malayalam will make the platform accessible to women in Kerala, helping foster local community engagement.
Odia Odia can help reach learners in Odisha and provide a comfortable learning experience in their native language.

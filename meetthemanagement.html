Our Services

Plumbing Services

Need a plumber for your home or office? With our app, you can easily request plumbing services. Whether it’s fixing leaks, installing pipes, or handling drainage issues, a skilled plumber will be at your doorstep promptly, ready to help.

Electrical Services

From fixing faulty wiring to installing electrical appliances, our team of certified electricians is just a few clicks away. Simply raise a service request through the app, and a nearby electrician will assist you based on your location.

Carpentry and Other Services

Our app also provides access to skilled carpenters, painters, and other essential service providers. No matter the task, raise a request, and a nearby expert will be sent to complete the job with professionalism and care.

How It Works

Step 1: Download the App

Download the Workplace app to get started. Our user-friendly app lets you easily select the services you need, from plumbing to electrical work and more.

Step 2: Set Your Location

Once you open the app, enter your location so we can send a skilled worker from your area to help you. The nearest professional will be dispatched based on your provided address.

Step 3: Request a Service

Whether you need immediate plumbing repairs or electrical installation, raise a request through the app. Our platform ensures that you’ll be connected to the right professional quickly.

Step 4: Skilled Worker at Your Doorstep

A skilled and verified professional will arrive at your location, equipped to handle the job with efficiency and expertise. Our workers are committed to providing quality service at affordable rates.

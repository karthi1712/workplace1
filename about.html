About

Welcome to WorkNear — your trusted platform for local services. Our mission is to empower skilled individuals by helping them connect with people in need of reliable and affordable home and personal services. Whether it’s plumbing, electrical work, tutoring, or other everyday tasks, WorkNear bridges the gap between skilled professionals and households looking for help.

Founded in 2024, WorkNear was built with a simple yet powerful idea: support local talent while making services accessible, transparent, and convenient for everyone. We provide a digital platform where service providers can showcase their skills and find meaningful work in their community, while users can easily search, book, and review the services they receive.

Our goal is to grow a vibrant, community-driven ecosystem where opportunity meets trust. With WorkNear, we’re not just offering services — we’re building a network of support, independence, and mutual growth.
